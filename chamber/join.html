Non-Profit

A non-profit membership does not cost you anything but your time and experience.

With a non-profit membership, you and your company are able to enjoy the benefits

of the many classes and networking extravaganzas.

Bronze

A Bronze membership only costs an annual fee of $5,000. You get the same benefits

as a non-profit membership but with even more opportunites for specialized training.

Silver

With a Silver Membership you get to be among the first to choose from our special events, receive even more key

training for you and your comapny, and increased discounts by 5%.

Gold

A Gold Membership gets you where you want to go, fast! Not only do you get specialized training, first pick at

special events, and increased discounts by 10%, you are also eligible to free advertising on our website!
