WDD 230

Overview

Site Name

I chose the name, Newport County Chamber of Commerce. I chose this name because I grew up in Newport, R.I. and know this town. I know the geography and the businesses. Not only do I know these things but I understand how living on an island that caters to tourists in the summer but still has to survive the rest of the year, can affect local businesses and commercial and personal real estate.

Site Purpose

Newport Chamber of Commerce will promote business and community development. It will be a resource for local businesses to create contacts and thrive. It will be a resource for information for those wanting to know more about the community.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be hosting? The chamber hosts a number of events throughout the year, such as After-Hours Networking, Innovative Discussions: [guest speaker], and the Local Crawl.
  • How can I become a member of the chamber? We need more community members who are commited to improving our community! There are three levels of membership. Become a champion in your community by becoming a non-Profit member. This is recommended for those small business with one locaton, looking for ways to grow. Silver members pay $1500/annually. This is recommended for businesses and corporations striving to make a difference in our community. Become an influencer for change in our community for $2250/annually. This is recommended for those companies that want to be directly engaged with the chamber in helping our community by becoming a Gold member. Click on the Join link for more information.
  • Is there a local directory that provides important phone numbers for newcomers to the area? Yes! Just click on the link that says Directory.
  • Is there a community guide to direct tourists to the best places to visit? There are many historical sites in Newport as well as award-winning dining and a one-of-a-kind shopping experience. Newport has it all! Click on the Discover link to find out more.

Wireframes

Home (large screen)

Home will have the navigation bar at the top. It will have a large picture of Newport with small business logos across the bottom. Underneath the picture is a call to action with community info and the weather following and lastly the footer at bottom.

Home (small screen)

Home will still have the navigation bar at the top but all the buttons will move to the right. The business logos will come under the navigation bar with the image of Newport following. The call to action is still under the image with the weather under that followed by the community info and footer.
